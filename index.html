What are Hackathons?

Demystifying hackathons for students from all backgrounds

Simple Definition

A hackathon is an event where people collaborate intensively to create projects that solve real-world problems. It's not just for programmers!

Who Can Participate?

Everyone! Designers, business students, medical students, artists, engineers - all fields are welcome and needed.
